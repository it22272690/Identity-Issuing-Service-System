A Persons Registration Office to Kurunegala...



    On February 12, 2017, President Maithripala Sirisena inaugurated two new sub-departments: the Department for Registration of Persons and the Department of Immigration and Emigration. This was done under the guidance of Prime Minister Ranil Wickramasinghe for a sustainable era. The provincial office in Kurunegala and Puttalam Districts aims to provide easy access to identity cards and resolve related issues for the people of the Wayamba Province. Schools, divisional secretariat offices, and state institutions can submit applications directly to the office. The office handles tasks such as postal-in, scanning, examination of documents, and transliteration. Identity card printing and related operations are carried out at the head office in Battaramulla. The people of the North Western Province can now obtain their identity cards quickly by submitting applications directly to the Kurunegala office or by post.




An amendment to the Number in the National Identity Card from 2016...




    Action has already been taken to amend the identity card number with effect from year 2016. The English letter appearing at the end of the identity card number will be removed and the number is amended to appear in 12 digits. The Department has taken action not to cause any inconvenience to the public by amending the number to appear in 12 digits and all institutions which are working with the identity card number have been informed about this change. The new number has been worked out without causing any confusion when comparing the already existing number with the new number. In addition if a duplicate has not been taken until now, existing number will continue to be valid. The new identity card with new number will carry the old identity card number on its reverse.
