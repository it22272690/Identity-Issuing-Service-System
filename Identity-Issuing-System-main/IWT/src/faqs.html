Popular Questions

✍ Can I obtain an identity card?


  • If you have completed 15 years of age and if you are a Sri Lankan citizen you can obtain an identity card.


✍ what are the situations to modify my identity card?


  • When you need to edit your name, address or any other personal information.
  • When your current appearance is different from the photograph in the identity card.
  • When your identity card is damaged after using it for a long time.
  • When a part of your identity card is damaged


✍ What are the documents I should submit to obtain a new NIC in place of a lost NIC ?


  • A certified police report to prove the loss.
  • A clear photograph obtained during last three months.


✍ How can I obtain a new identity card?


  • Fill the New NIC form with necessary details.
  • Attach a photocopy of the birth certificate certified by Grama Niladhari.
  • A clear photograph obtained during last three months.
